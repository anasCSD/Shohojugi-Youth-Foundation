Who We Are

The Shohojugi Youth Foundation is a volunteer-driven charity organization focused on providing support to underprivileged communities. We believe in the power of youth to bring about meaningful change.

From distributing food and clothes to organizing educational programs, we strive to make a lasting impact on society.
